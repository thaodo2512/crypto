# SS-10: Signal 2 — Leverage Positioning

**Master Spec Sections:** §4.2
**Dependencies:** SS-01, SS-03
**Estimated LOC:** ~180

## Scope

Computes Composite Signal 2 (Leverage Positioning) — a directional score in [-1, +1] answering "How are leveraged traders positioned, and is the crowd overextended?" Combines funding rate (contrarian at extremes), OI-price regime, smart vs. retail divergence, and taker aggression, with an internal consistency multiplier. Does NOT collect futures data (SS-03) or combine with other signals (SS-15).

## Key Requirements

1. Component 1 — Funding Rate (weight `leverage_positioning.funding_weight`): Contrarian at extremes, momentum at moderate. Maps funding rate to score using config thresholds.
2. Component 2 — OI-Price Regime (weight `leverage_positioning.oi_price_weight`): Uses regime from `futures_snapshot` table. NEW_LONGS→+0.8, NEW_SHORTS→-0.8, LONG_CLOSING→-0.3, SHORT_CLOSING→+0.3.
3. Component 3 — Smart vs. Retail Divergence (weight `leverage_positioning.smart_retail_weight`): Compares top_ls_ratio vs global_ls_ratio. Top long + retail short → +0.8, opposite → -0.8, aligned → ±0.2.
4. Component 4 — Taker Aggression (weight `leverage_positioning.taker_weight`): `score = clip((taker_ratio - 1.0) × taker_multiplier, -1, +1)`.
5. Internal Consistency Check: 3+ same direction → ×1.3, 2 same + 0 opposite → ×1.1, 2+ positive AND 2+ negative → ×0.5, otherwise ×0.8.
6. Final = `clip(weighted_sum × consistency_multiplier, -1, +1)`.

## Acceptance Criteria
