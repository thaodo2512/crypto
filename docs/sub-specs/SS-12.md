# SS-12: Signal 4 — Mean Reversion

**Master Spec Sections:** §4.4
**Dependencies:** SS-01, SS-02, SS-05
**Estimated LOC:** ~150

## Scope

Computes Composite Signal 4 (Mean Reversion) — a directional score in [-1, +1] answering "Is the market overextended and due for a pullback or bounce?" Acts as a brake on momentum signals. Combines RSI, price vs. VWAP, futures basis, Fear & Greed, and Bollinger Band position. Does NOT collect data (SS-02, SS-05) or combine with other signals (SS-15).

## Key Requirements

1. Component 1 — RSI (weight `mean_reversion.rsi_weight`): Maps RSI(14) to score per §4.4 thresholds using config values.
2. Component 2 — Price vs. VWAP (weight `mean_reversion.vwap_weight`): `distance_pct = (price - vwap) / vwap × 100`. Maps to score per §4.4.
3. Component 3 — Futures Basis (weight `mean_reversion.basis_weight`): Uses `basis_pct` from futures_snapshot. Maps annualized basis to score per §4.4.
4. Component 4 — Fear & Greed (weight `mean_reversion.fear_greed_weight`): Uses F&G from daily_snapshot. Maps to score per §4.4.
5. Component 5 — Bollinger Band Position (weight `mean_reversion.bollinger_weight`): `bb_position = (price - bb_lower) / (bb_upper - bb_lower)`. Maps to score per §4.4.
6. Final = `clip(C1×w1 + C2×w2 + C3×w3 + C4×w4 + C5×w5, -1, +1)`.
